Our leasing process

Leasing a suite at Courthouse Square is straightforward. Here's the sequence from first tour to signed lease.

  1. 1

    Tour the space

    In person or virtually. We'll walk you through the available suites and the building's shared spaces.

  2. 2

    Send us a Letter of Intent

    It tells us the suite you want, your proposed start date, term length, and any special needs. Email the completed Part I to CHSLeasing@bangasser.com along with a refundable $100 Good Faith Deposit — credited toward your first month's rent if we sign, refunded in full within three business days if we don't.

  3. 3

    We review and respond

    Usually within a few business days. We run standard background and credit checks (authorized in the LOI) and either confirm, ask clarifying questions, or propose adjustments.

  4. 4

    Sign and move in

    The signed lease is the same standard lease you can read below, with your specific terms filled in.

Why is the lease public?

We post our standard lease publicly because no one should have to commit before they've seen the fine print. Read it. Share it with your attorney. Show up to the conversation already informed.
